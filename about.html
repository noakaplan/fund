Project 1: Hue Poster

Students curate graphic content for a poster about a specific hue.

Software: Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator

  • Research the chemical origins and cultural meanings of a particular color.
  • Create a monotone poster integrating research and color values (HSB, RGB, CMYK, HEX).
  • Make sure to include citations (Chicago style citations).
  • Posters should be printed on tabloid size paper.

    Project 2: Interaction of Color Playing Cards

    Students create color studies based on Joseph Albers Interaction of Color. The students then edit and refine the studies until they develop a system that will function in gameplay.

    Software: Adobe Illustrator and InDesign

    Phase 1

    • 16 color step compositions
    • 16 false transparency compositions
    • 16 vibrating color compositions
    • 16 subtractive color compositions
    • 16 reversed ground compositions

    Phase 2

    • functional system development
    • layout designs
    • test prints
    • final designs
